# From Individual AI Use to Collaborative AI (CollabAI)

## The Limits of Solo Prompting

Describe the typical early-stage AI usage:

- Individual experimenting with ChatGPT
- Ad hoc prompting
- Personal productivity boosts
- Isolated usage

Problem:

- Knowledge stays individual
- No shared learning
- No business alignment
- No systematic improvement

--- 

## CollabAI: AI as a Team Member

Introduce the CollabAI concept:

- Co-located teams
- Business and developers together
- Real interviews
- Real customer context
- AI used during collaboration, not after

Important principles:

- AI supports thinking, not replaces it
- AI is present in workshops
- AI accelerates ideation and validation
- AI documents and structures knowledge in real time

Core argument:

>AI maturity is not about technical skill — it is about collective working methods.

You can emphasize:

- Teams develop their own AI patterns.
- Prompt engineering is less important than context engineering.
- Learning happens through iterative practice.
