# 1. Introduction: AI Is Not a Tool — It’s a Capability Shift

**Purpose:** Set the tone.

Key message:

- AI is not “just another tool.”
- It changes how we think, collaborate, design, and build.
- The organizations that succeed are not those that attend AI courses — but those that integrate AI into daily work.

You can introduce your core thesis:

>You don’t learn AI from training programs. You learn it by working with it — in real problems, in real teams, with business context.
